Introduction

Deltarune is an episodic role-playing game developed and written by Toby Fox, serving as a parallel story to Undertale. Chapters 1 & 2 were released for free (Oct 31, 2018 / Sep 17,2021), while Chapters 3 & 4 launched as a paid bundle on June 5, 2025 across PC, Mac, Nintendo Switch, Switch 2, PS4, and PS5. Future chapters will be added for free updates to players who own Chapters 1-4

Game Story

The player controls Kris, a non-binary human teen from Hometown, who unexpectedly becomes a hero by entering a "Dark World" alongside classmates Susie and Ralsei.

Each chapter has a self-contained storyline centered around sealing a Dark Fountain, while also building the larger narrative arc across chapters

Chapter 1 introduces the Dark World's first throne and characters like Lancer and King. Chapters 3-4 offer broader worlds like cyber-themed areas, game-show segments, and the absurd humor Toby Fox is known for
