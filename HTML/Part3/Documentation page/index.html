Introduction

Deltarune is an episodic role-playing game developed and written by Toby Fox, serving as a parallel story to Undertale. Chapters 1 & 2 were released for free (Oct 31, 2018 / Sep 17,2021), while Chapters 3 & 4 launched as a paid bundle on June 5, 2025 across PC, Mac, Nintendo Switch, Switch 2, PS4, and PS5. Future chapters will be added for free updates to players who own Chapters 1-4

What You Should Already Know

    Familiarity with Undertale mechanics (e.g. compositional elements, bullet-dodging, moral combat) helps comprehension.

    Basic RPG terms: HP, ATK, DEF, TP, EXP, and character levels—some adapted from Undertale.

    Awareness of chapter-based episodic narrative structure, including branching choices and different routes like the Weird Route

Game Story

    The player controls Kris, a non-binary human teen from Hometown, who unexpectedly becomes a hero by entering a "Dark World" alongside classmates Susie and Ralsei.

    Each chapter has a self-contained storyline centered around sealing a Dark Fountain, while also building the larger narrative arc across chapters

    Chapter 1 introduces the Dark World's first throne and characters like Lancer and King. Chapters 2-4 offer broader worlds like cyber-themed areas, game-show segments, and the absurd humor Toby Fox is known for

Key Mechanics

    Real-time overworld: you can run by holding Cancel, or enable Auto-Run in Dark World config settings

    Save points in each chapter fully restore HP and act as checkpoint markers.

    The game records Completion Data which carries items, characters recruited, and choices into subsequent chapters. If starting fresh, the game provides a default state with default gear and assumed pacifist conduct

    Combat: turn-based with options to Fight, Act, Item, Magic, Spare, or Defend. Enemies can be fought, spared, or scared off—EXPerence is not earned in Chapter 1 because no enemies die, but EXP starts appearing from Chapter 2 onward, especially on Weird Routes

    Tension Points (TP) build when you graze/dodge attacks or use Defend—used to cast spells or powerful acts

Characters

    Kris: The silent protagonist controlled indirectly, reacts in ways that may surprise the player—portrayed distinctly from a blank avatar

    Susie: A rebellious monster character who develops through forced teamwork and chaotic combat style

    Ralsei: The gentle Dark World prince who encourages pacifism and offers healing and support magic.

    Noelle: A light-world classmate who joins the party in Chapter 2. She wields ice magic (IceShock, SnowGrave) and plays a key role in Weird Route progression and Chapter 4 exploration

    Berdly: Noelle's friend and rival, whose prideful intellect gradually softens. Though not a full-time party member, Berdly becomes an ally by the end of Chapter 2 and recurs in Chapter 4

Technical information

    Engine: GameMaker Studio 2; developed and primarily directed by Toby Fox with art by Temmie Chang

    Stats system:

  • HP: non-lethal enemy defeat, revived with items or spells.
  • ATK / DEF: Attack power and defense calculation (defending reduces damage further; defense triples when calculating incoming damage)
  • EXP / LV: Experience is hidden or inactive early on; visible growth begins Chapter 2 or on certain routes. No experience is earned when enemies flee or battles end prematurely.

    • Config options: text skip, auto-run, fullscreen toggle (F4 on PC), instant text box advance via holding Cancel or Menu button

    Party Attacks & Accuracy

  • Damage is computed as: round((ATK x Accuracy)/20 - 3 x Enemy DEF)
  • A miss still deals damage if defense is negative
