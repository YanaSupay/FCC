"Tell you what, you big shot!"

Here's a time line of Spamton's:

Early life:
  • Originally existed as an unsuccessful spam email Addison, overlooked by Lightners and never able to catch a break
  • Despite his lack of success, he always proclaimed to his fellow Addisons that he would be a "big shot" one day
Rise to Fame:
  • Was once a random salesman who nobody noticed, until one phone call from someone made him into a big shot who was popular enough to get a room in Queen's Mansion
  • Became successful enough to run his own business
  • This lead to his old friends, the Addisons, abandoning him out of nothing but envy
The Fall:
  • Lost his mysterious benefactor and fell from grace
  • Appeared to have lost his mind upon becoming broke and homeless or through the interactions with the mysterious caller that made him popular
  • Became desperate to regain his former glory
Present Day:
  • Encountered in Cyber City where he is fought as a miniboss
  • Still tries to sell his products to anyone who will listen, including the player
  • Continues to proclaim that he will be a "big shot" again one day
  • Has deep secrets and hidden knowledge, and is in fact the bonus boss of the chapter in his second form, Spamton NEO

Character traits

  • Speech becomes a rapid and confusing mix of brazen deal-making, garbled nonsequiturs from online chain messages and advertisements, and out-of-context ramblings about his past
  • Resembles a salesman's character, often seen trying to sell something or make a deal
  • Has a tendency to use all-caps and bold text in his speech, giving him a frantic and erratic personality
  • Has a small body, big eyes, and is usually surrounded by a glitchy, digital-like aura. His design is reminiscent of a puppet or a toy
